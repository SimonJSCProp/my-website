Why Us

The JSC Difference

Most buyers agents have a conflict of interest they won't tell you about. Here's what makes JSC structurally different — and why it matters.

Veteran-Owned, Integrity-Led

JSC was co-founded by two brothers who served. Simon spent 11 years in the Australian Army — Helicopter Mechanic, Combat Fitness Leader, Combat First Aider — discharging as a Corporal in 2022. Jacob served in the Royal Australian Air Force before leaving to pursue financial freedom through property, which he's been actively investing in since 2015. The discipline, straight-talking, and people-first values from their service run through every client engagement.

No Kickbacks. No Commissions. No BS.

Some operators in this industry receive $30,000 or more from builders and developers for every property they recommend — then present it as independent guidance. Think about what that does to their advice. JSC has never accepted a dollar from any developer, builder, or vendor. Not once. Our only income is the fixed fee you pay us — so the only outcome we're optimising for is yours.

The Capital Compounder™ Strategy

Our proprietary acquisition framework identifies established properties in high-growth corridors before the market catches up — often off-market. It's a repeatable, systematic process built on 80-point due diligence and 100+ active agent relationships across QLD.

Below Market Value — Always the Target

We negotiate with data, comparable sales, and no emotional attachment. Most JSC clients purchase below the asking price — sometimes significantly. That instant equity is your buffer, your safety net, and your head start on growth.

A Track Record You Can Verify

Zero losses. 30%+ average capital growth across the portfolio — and that figure includes properties we've only just settled, which drags the average down significantly. The true long-term number is materially higher. We publish our results because no competitor can manufacture this record.

Our Business Model Runs on Your Results

Our only incentive is to find you the very best performing property we can — because a client who builds real wealth comes back. Many JSC clients have come back to buy multiple properties with us over time, using equity from each to fund the next. That's the business model: do excellent work, earn repeat clients.
