The Basics

What Is a Buyers Agent — and Why Does It Matter?

A buyers agent (or buyers advocate) represents the buyer in a property transaction. Unlike a traditional real estate agent who works for the vendor and maximises the sale price, we work exclusively for you and focus on securing the right property at the lowest possible price.

In a market where the vendor has professional representation, negotiation expertise, and information advantages — going it alone puts you at a structural disadvantage before you even start. JSC levels the playing field.

The result? Properties purchased at or below market value. Time saved. Stress eliminated. Better long-term outcomes.

Our Process

What Working With JSC Looks Like

From first conversation to keys in hand — here's every step of the journey.

1

Free Discovery Call

We start with a 30-minute call to understand your situation — budget, goals, timeline, and whether you're investing or owner-occupying. No obligation. Just straight talk about what's possible.

2

Deep-Dive Strategy Session

We analyse your financial position, borrowing capacity, and property goals in detail. We'll tell you exactly what to look for, which markets suit your strategy, and what a realistic target property looks like.

3

Property Brief & Search Criteria

We build a precise property brief — property type, suburb criteria, price range, must-have features, and deal-breakers. This drives a targeted search, not a scatter-gun one.

4

Market Search — On & Off Market

We search on-market listings, agent networks, and off-market opportunities. We inspect every shortlisted property physically and apply our due diligence checklist before you're ever involved.

5

Shortlist Presentation

We present only properties that genuinely meet your brief. No filler. Each recommendation comes with our full analysis, comparable sales data, and a clear recommendation on price.

6

Negotiation & Offer

We negotiate hard with data, comparable evidence, and no emotional attachment. Our goal is always to secure the property at or below market value — and we don't accept the first counter-offer without a fight.

7

Contracts, Inspections & Settlement

Once under contract, we coordinate building and pest inspections, conveyancers, and any other professionals needed. We stay involved all the way to settlement day so nothing falls through the cracks.

8

Ongoing Portfolio Reviews

Every six months, we review your property's performance with real market valuations, rental data, and updated strategy recommendations. You'll always know exactly where you stand and what to do next.

Transparent Pricing

A Fixed Fee. No Surprises. No Conflicts.

Some in this industry don't charge you directly at all — they're paid $30,000 or more per property by the developer who built it. If someone recommending a property stands to pocket that kind of money from the other side of the deal, ask yourself: who are they actually working for?

Others charge a percentage of your purchase price — which means the more you spend, the more they earn. That's not a service model. That's a misaligned incentive with a business card.

JSC charges one clear, fixed fee agreed upfront. No commissions. No developer kickbacks. No percentage of your purchase price. What you pay is what we earn — and our fee is the same whether you buy at $400K or $900K.

Our fee typically pays for itself many times over through the price we negotiate. Clients regularly buy $50K–$100K below market — that's day-one equity before we've even handed over the keys.

Every situation is different. Book your free Deep Dive Strategy Session and we'll give you a fixed quote with no obligation to proceed.
