Common Questions

Is the Discovery Call really free?

+

Yes, completely. The Discovery Call is a genuine no-obligation conversation. We use it to understand your goals and determine whether we're the right fit for you. There's no pressure and no pitch — if we can't add value, we'll tell you.

Do I need to be in QLD to use JSC?

+

Not at all. Many of our clients are interstate or overseas investors who want QLD exposure without having to physically be there. We handle everything on the ground — inspections, negotiations, due diligence — so location is never a barrier.

How do you make money — and could there be conflicts of interest?

+

We charge a fixed fee agreed with you upfront — that's it. We do not accept kickbacks, referral fees, or commissions from developers, vendors, or agents. Many agencies that call themselves "buyers agents" are secretly paid by developers to push stock — that's a fundamental conflict of interest. Our only incentive is to get you the best possible property at the best possible price. No conflicts, full stop.

What if you can't find the right property?

+

If we can't find something that genuinely meets our criteria for your situation, we'll tell you — clearly and early. We'd rather lose an engagement than put a client into the wrong property. That's not a risk to you; it's how we protect our track record and our reputation. No JSC client has ever lost money, and we intend to keep it that way.

How long does it take to find and secure a property?

+

It varies. Our fastest ever was 4 days — the right property appeared and we moved immediately. The sweet spot for most clients is 6–8 weeks from engagement to signed contract. We don't rush, and we don't drag our feet — we wait for the right property at the right price, then move decisively when it appears.

What happens after settlement?

+

Our relationship doesn't end at settlement. Every six months we review your property's performance with real market valuations, rental income data, and a forward strategy. We also help you plan your next move — whether that's refinancing to access equity, buying another property, or just monitoring the current one.

Can you help with my first home purchase?

+

Absolutely. First home buyers are one of our most common client types. We know the process can be overwhelming — inspections, auctions, negotiations, and paperwork all at once. We simplify everything, protect you from overpaying, and make sure you start your homeownership journey in the strongest possible position.
